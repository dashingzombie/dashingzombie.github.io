Presented the **Insect Eavesdropper**, an end-to-end bioacoustic pipeline combining contact-microphone acquisition, signal preprocessing, self-supervised feature extraction, and classification. The system achieved **96% precision** on in-plant pest detection; the pitch won first place and a **$5,000 award**.
