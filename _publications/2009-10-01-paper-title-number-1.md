We calibrated VL53L3CX and VL6180X time-of-flight sensors using ROS, Gazebo, and a UR5 robot arm. The method achieved positional accuracy of 3.18 mm and 7.29 mm and orientation accuracy of 0.61° and 2.01°. Validation through 3D scene reconstruction produced sub-2 mm residual errors on previously unseen planar objects.
